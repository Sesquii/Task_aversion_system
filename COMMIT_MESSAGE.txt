feat(popups): add momentum, break, score milestone, and weekly summary popups

Added four new popup triggers to provide feedback and encouragement at key moments.

## Popup Triggers Added

### Momentum Popup (Trigger 4.1)
- Fires when user completes exactly 5 tasks in last 24 hours
- Only shows if momentum_factor >= 0.7 (high momentum)
- Tiered messaging based on frequency
- Options: "Keep Going" or "Take a Break"
- Cooldown: 24 hours (once per day max)

### Take a Break Popup (Trigger 1.1)
- Fires when user has been working for 4+ hours with minimal breaks
- "Significant idle time" = 30+ minutes between tasks
- If average gap < 30 minutes over 4+ hours, suggests break needed
- Tiered messaging based on frequency
- Options: "Take a Break" or "Keep Going"
- Cooldown: 24 hours (once per day max)

### Score Milestone Popup (Trigger 6.1)
- Fires on first task completion of the day if yesterday achieved a score milestone
- Checks productivity, execution, grit, and composite scores
- Shows milestone closest to all-time best (prioritized)
- Milestones: All-time best or top 10 scores
- Tiered messaging based on frequency
- Options: "Keep Going" or "View Scores"
- Cooldown: 24 hours (once per day max)
- Only one score milestone popup per day (best one shown)

### Weekly Progress Summary Popup (Trigger 5.1)
- Fires on first task completion of the configured day of week (default: Monday)
- Configurable via user preferences (0=Monday, 6=Sunday)
- Shows weekly progress summary for the last 7 days:
  - Tasks completed
  - Days active
  - Average scores (productivity, execution, grit)
  - Best day productivity score
  - Total work time
  - Trend indicators (up/down/stable)
- Tiered messaging based on frequency
- Options: "Keep Going" or "View Details"
- Cooldown: 7 days (once per week max)
